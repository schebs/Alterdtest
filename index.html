Pierce Alter

Pierce Alter founded Alter'D Workshop with a singular vision: to create handmade jewelry that transcends trends and becomes a lasting part of your story. With years of experience in fine jewelry design, Pierce approaches each commission as a unique collaboration between artisan and client.

From initial concept to final polish, every detail is considered, every line refined. The result is not just jewelry, but wearable art that captures your vision in precious metal and stone.

Specializing in handmade custom rings, pendants, and earrings, Pierce brings your ideas to life with uncompromising quality and meticulous attention to detail.
